Asktopedia is a web app similar to Stack Overflow. In this application, all technical users can ask and clear their doubts with other registered developers.

Unique features in this app:

Developers can communicate and arrange meetups between them.
Developers who post answers can also upload related material and documents for further reference.
The platform provides support through sessions and online meetings.
Our app gives various badges and appreciation rewards to developers once they earn certain badges. this is my project abstract so make fullyfunctional website .here are some cautions :1. react app must be created using vite only. 2.only external css are acceptable 3.backend must connectect with front end as well as database . 4. website must be responsive
